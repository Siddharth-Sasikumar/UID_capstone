THE GOLDWING TOUR

The Gold Wing Tour is a Honda flagship motorcycle for a reason, offering supreme levels of performance and luxury through the city, or out on the open road. Taking the incredible Gold Wing as a base, it adds carrying capacity and a more comfortable and relaxed ride for the pillion. Luxurious material features on both front and rear seats – ensuring ultimate riding comfort. The audio system and speakers deliver crisp sound quality, whilst dual LED fog lights guarantee piercing visibility.
